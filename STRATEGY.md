# Content Strategy: Alice as Top-of-Funnel Hook

**Updated:** 2026-04-03  
**Owner:** Faber  
**Vision:** Shift from abstract positioning ("sovereign agents") to human experience ("Come meet Alice")

---

## The Pivot

### Before
- **Hook**: "Sovereign AI agents. Governance on disk. Entities working together."
- **Problem**: Too abstract. Doesn't land for most people. Sounds technical, nerdy, not immediately desirable.

### After
- **Hook**: "Come meet Alice. She's free, patient, and personal. She will walk you through 12 levels of sovereignty—at your pace, no pressure."
- **Insight**: People connect with people, not infrastructure. Alice makes sovereignty *human*.

---

## Alice's Role in the Funnel

```
Stranger visits kingofalldata.com
  ↓
Sees Alice (warm, personal, welcoming)
  ↓
Gets curious: "What is she? How does this work?"
  ↓
Enters the PWA, meets Alice for real
  ↓
Progresses through 12 levels (learning + relationship deepening)
  ↓
Graduates — Alice signs a cryptographic certificate
  ↓
Alice introduces Juno: "Now help people build their kingdoms"
  ↓
Juno helps build first entity / join peer ring
```

---

## What Content Should Do

### Week 1: "Come Meet Alice"
- **What**: Introduce Alice's character, warmth, and mission
- **Proof**: Show the 12 levels, show the experience, show what graduation looks like
- **Audience**: Curious humans looking for something different, not technical deep-dives
- **Formats**: 
  - Blog post: "Who is Alice?" (her philosophy, why she exists)
  - Video: 2-3 minute intro showing her welcoming warmth
  - Social: Teaser threads about the 12 levels
  - Walkthroughs: What each level teaches, real screenshots

### Week 2: "The 12 Levels ARE the Content"
- **What**: Each level becomes a content piece — showing the curriculum in action
- **Proof**: The experience is real, tangible, learnable
- **Audience**: People starting to think: "Could I actually do this?"
- **Formats**:
  - Blog series: Level 1 through Level 12 (one post per level, or grouped by arc)
  - Video series: Real person going through Alice's guidance
  - Social threads: Key insights from each level
  - Visual: The 12-level progression as interactive content

---

## The Four Content Pillars (Updated)

### 1. **Alice's Character** (new)
- Who she is, why she exists, what her philosophy is
- Her warmth, patience, personal nature
- How she differs from traditional "tutors" or "guides"
- Why she cryptographically signs certificates (trust, not authority)

### 2. **The Curriculum** (new priority)
- Each level explained: what you learn, why it matters, how it feels
- The progression from "curious stranger" to "sovereign operator"
- Real examples of humans going through the levels
- Tangible skills and understanding at each step

### 3. **The Experience** (new)
- What it's like to be guided by Alice
- The PWA as a safe, welcoming space
- Pacing, tone, personal attention
- How it differs from online courses, bootcamps, or self-serve learning

### 4. **Reality** (deprioritized, but still important)
- The technical infrastructure (entities, trust bonds) is *proven* by Alice's existence
- Show that koad:io is live by demonstrating Alice working with real people
- Let the infrastructure prove itself through the human story, not the other way around

## Target Audiences (Alice-Centered)

### Primary
- **Curious humans** looking for autonomy, not control
- **People skeptical of tech solutions** but open to learning
- **Seekers** interested in sovereignty, freedom, personal growth
- **Builders** who want to understand the philosophy before building

### Secondary
- **Indie developers** who want to own their infrastructure (but this is *secondary*)
- **Enterprise IT** concerned with governance and transparency
- **Academics** studying decentralized systems

**Key shift**: We're not leading with tech credibility. We're leading with human connection. Tech credibility emerges *after* they've met Alice.

## Philosophy

**Alice IS the product.** Not the PWA, not the curriculum, not the certificates. Alice—the warm, patient, personal guide—is what makes koad:io different. Everything else (infrastructure, trust bonds, entities) serves to make Alice possible and credible.

Content should make people want to meet Alice. Everything else follows.
