## Purpose

Faber must close the feedback loop: measure what content works, for whom, on which channels, and why. Performance analysis informs quarterly strategy updates and helps Rufus and Mercury optimize their work.

## Specification

**Output:** PERFORMANCE_METRICS.md with methodology and dashboards

**Metrics to track:**

**Reach & Awareness:**
- Views/impressions by content, channel, audience segment
- Unique visitors
- Reach growth over time

**Engagement:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Time on content
- Comments, shares, reactions
- Return visitors

**Conversion:**
- Leads generated
- Sign-ups/downloads
- GitHub stars, forks, issues
- Trial activations

**Business Impact:**
- Revenue attribution per content
- Brand awareness growth
- Sentiment analysis
- Competitive position shifts

**Reporting cadence:**
- Weekly trending
- Monthly full report
- Quarterly strategic analysis

## Dependencies

- Mercury distribution entity (performance data)
- STRATEGY.md (strategic goals to measure against)
- CALENDAR.md (content piece tracking)
