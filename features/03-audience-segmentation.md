# Feature: Audience Segmentation & Messaging

**Status:** Planned (unbuilt)  
**Owner:** Faber  
**Scope:** Define target audiences and craft messaging frameworks

## Description

Identify and profile target audiences for koad:io ecosystem, develop tailored messaging strategies for each segment.

## Capability

**What:** Create audience segment profiles and messaging frameworks that align with Iris brand direction

**For whom:** Rufus (production messaging), Mercury (distribution targeting), Iris (brand alignment)

**Inputs needed:**
- Market research from Sibyl
- Brand voice/positioning from Iris
- Distribution channel performance from Mercury

**Output:**
- AUDIENCE_SEGMENTS.md — Detailed segment profiles
- Messaging frameworks per segment
- Content angle recommendations
- Channel-audience mapping

## Audience Segments (Hypothetical)

1. **Platform Adopters** — Companies building AI entities
   - Pain point: How do we run AI as a business?
   - Messaging angle: Sovereignty, control, long-term ownership
   - Channels: Dev newsletters, HN, GitHub, technical blogs

2. **Framework Builders** — Open-source framework maintainers
   - Pain point: How do I create sustainable ecosystem?
   - Messaging angle: Community economics, entity sales model
   - Channels: Framework communities, Twitter, conferences

3. **AI Researchers** — Academic/research institutions
   - Pain point: How do I operationalize AI research?
   - Messaging angle: Entity architectures, sovereign agents
   - Channels: Research papers, academic Twitter, workshops

4. **Enterprises** — Established companies needing AI capabilities
   - Pain point: Building custom AI without vendor risk
   - Messaging angle: Control, integration, rapid deployment
   - Channels: Enterprise newsletters, LinkedIn, trade shows

5. **Hobbyists & Makers** — Individual developers, small teams
   - Pain point: How do I build/run small AI business?
   - Messaging angle: Accessible, fun, low-cost, creative
   - Channels: Twitter, Reddit, Discord, blogs

## How It Works

1. Research market from Sibyl, brand from Iris
2. Define 4-6 core audience segments
3. Profile each: pain points, goals, channels, tone
4. Develop messaging framework per segment
5. Create content angle recommendations
6. Map to distribution channels
7. Brief Rufus with segment-specific messaging angles
8. Update quarterly based on performance
