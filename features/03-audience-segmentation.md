## Purpose

Faber must identify and profile the different audiences for koad:io, then develop tailored messaging strategies for each segment. This ensures content resonates with who it's made for and that Rufus knows how to message each audience.

## Specification

**Output:** AUDIENCE_SEGMENTS.md with segment profiles

**Per-segment documentation:**
- **Audience name & description** — Who are they, what do they do
- **Pain points & goals** — What problems do they face, what do they want
- **Key messaging angles** — How to position koad:io to this segment (3-5 messages)
- **Content types** — What formats work for this audience
- **Tone & voice** — How to talk to them (formal, casual, technical, accessible, etc.)
- **Channels** — Where they spend time (social media, forums, conferences, etc.)
- **Success metrics** — How to measure if messaging resonates

**Expected segments:**
- Platform adopters (companies building AI entities)
- Framework builders (open-source maintainers)
- AI researchers (academic/research institutions)
- Enterprise buyers (established companies)
- Developers & makers (individuals, small teams)
