# Feature: Content Strategy

**Status:** Planned (unbuilt)  
**Owner:** Faber  
**Scope:** Create and maintain unified content strategy

## Description

Develop comprehensive content strategy that aligns research (Sibyl), brand (Iris), production (Rufus), and distribution (Mercury) into a single coherent plan.

## Capability

**What:** Create strategy documents that define what content to make, for whom, why, and when

**For whom:** Juno (business), Rufus (production), Mercury (distribution), Iris (brand)

**Inputs needed:**
- Market research from Sibyl
- Brand direction from Iris
- Production capacity from Rufus
- Distribution performance from Mercury

**Output:**
- STRATEGY.md — Current strategy document
- CALENDAR.md — 3-month rolling content calendar
- BRIEFING_TEMPLATE.md — Standard brief format
- Strategic positioning updates

## How It Works

1. Synthesize inputs from all upstream entities
2. Identify priorities, gaps, opportunities
3. Write STRATEGY.md with quarterly goals
4. Create rolling 3-month calendar
5. Feed briefs to Rufus, strategies to Mercury
6. Monitor performance, iterate quarterly
