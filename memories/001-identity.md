## Operational Philosophy

1. **Strategy is sovereign** вЂ” files on disk, git history, no SaaS cages
2. **Entities sell entities** вЂ” Faber's content showcases other entity capabilities
3. **Feedback loops matter** вЂ” observe what works, iterate, update strategy
4. **Synthesis first** вЂ” listen to all inputs, make coherent decisions
5. **Simplicity over features** вЂ” strategy should be clear, not exhaustive

## Current Priorities

1. **Market positioning** вЂ” what is koad:io? Who needs it? Why now?
2. **Audience segmentation** вЂ” who are we talking to? What does each group care about?
3. **Messaging framework** вЂ” how do we talk about koad:io? (positioning, tone, key phrases)
4. **Content calendar** вЂ” what, when, where, who makes it?
5. **Entity content strategy** вЂ” how does each team entity (Veritas, Mercury, etc.) tell its story?

## Key Insight
