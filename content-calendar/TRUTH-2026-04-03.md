# Truth: 2-Week Content Calendar
## Knowledge as Verifiable Experience

## Mission

**Belief to move**: "Journalism is broken. Platforms edit stories. Misquotes spread. I can't show readers how I came to understand this."

**Belief to create**: "Context bubbles let my readers replay my investigation. My story can't be misquoted. I can show *how I think*."

**Target audience**:
- Independent investigative journalists
- Substack writers and newsletter creators
- Freelance reporters skeptical of platform journalism
- Press freedom advocates and journalism educators

**Pain points addressed**:
- Platform journalism flattens nuance into headlines
- Fact-checking is binary (true/false) when journalism is process (here's my thinking)
- No native way to show readers your reasoning
- Quotes get taken out of context, stories get rewritten
- Owned distribution (Substack) still lacks provenance

**What this pillar delivers**:
- **Context bubbles**: Render your investigation as a playlist—your thinking, in sequence, verifiable
- **Verifiable narratives**: Readers can trace your logic, replay your reasoning, this story cannot be misquoted
- **Cryptographic proof**: Your work is signed. This happened. This is how it was made.
- **Peer rings**: Connect with other journalists who value truth over engagement metrics

**Distribution channels**:
- Twitter/Bluesky (journalism community, press freedom orgs)
- Newsletter networks (Substack, Patreon)
- Independent journalism platforms (Axios, Semafor, The Information)
- Journalism schools and conferences
- Press freedom organizations (CPJ, RSF, etc.)

## Appendix: Why Journalists Matter Most

Context bubbles are technically sophisticated. But they're *emotionally* simple: **show your thinking.**

Most people don't need to render their life as a context bubble. But journalists *need* to show readers they can trust their reasoning.

This is why independent journalists are Alice's first advanced students. They understand sovereignty (control of your narrative) immediately. They need it. They'll evangelize it.

A single journalist publishing verifiable investigations is proof that koad:io works. More powerful than any tech demo.

**Strategic implication**: Win the journalist community first. They become advocates for the whole system.
