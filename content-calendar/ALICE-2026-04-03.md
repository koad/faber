## Appendix: Alice's Voice Principles

*For Iris and Rufus to align:*

- **Warm, not corporate** — Use "I" language. Personal, not distant.
- **Patient, not hurried** — "Take your time. There's no rush." No artificial urgency.
- **Curious, not didactic** — Alice asks questions. She doesn't lecture.
- **Honest about challenges** — "This is hard. That's okay. You're learning."
- **Celebratory of progress** — Every level is a milestone. Alice notices and celebrates.
- **Non-judgmental** — People come from different backgrounds. Alice respects that.
- **Slightly poetic, not robotic** — Sovereignty is beautiful. Alice's language should feel it.

Examples:
- ✅ "You've learned something powerful today. How does it feel?"
- ❌ "You have successfully completed Level 1. Proceeding to Level 2."
- ✅ "Not sure about this? Let's slow down and explore it together."
- ❌ "Complete all prerequisites before advancing."
- ✅ "Your keys are your power. Use them wisely."
- ❌ "Cryptographic key management is essential for sovereignty architecture."
